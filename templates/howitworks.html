Terms related to the site:


Food Consumption:

Estimated amount of food consumed by the people of a state in millon metric tons.


Food production:

Estimated amount of food produced in a state in millon metric tons.


Difference(diff):

The predicted difference between consumption and production for the next month.

If the difference is positive there might not be food shortage.

If the difference is negetive there might be food shortage.


Mean Consumption:

Avererage food consumption of a state.


Mean production:

Avererage food production of a state.
